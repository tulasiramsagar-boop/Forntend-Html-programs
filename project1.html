Animal (2023 Indian Film)
Animal (2023), directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga, was a major commercial success but generated significant controversy for its depictions of violence and toxic masculinity. 
Plot: The film revolves around the complex and abusive relationship between Ranvijay Singh (Ranbir Kapoor), the son of a wealthy industrialist Balbir Singh (Anil Kapoor), and his emotionally distant father. After an assassination attempt on Balbir, Ranvijay goes on a violent rampage to find and eliminate the culprits, eventually uncovering a fierce gang war and a family feud with his cousin Abrar Haque (Bobby Deol).
Cast: The film stars Ranbir Kapoor, Anil Kapoor, Bobby Deol, and Rashmika Mandanna in lead roles, with Triptii Dimri also appearing in a significant role.
Reception: The movie received a mixed-to-negative critical reception. Reviewers praised the performances (particularly Kapoor's), the technical aspects like cinematography and background music, and the action sequences. However, it faced severe criticism for its themes, graphic violence, misogyny, and excessive runtime of over three hours.
